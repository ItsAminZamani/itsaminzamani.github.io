RandomLettersBot

RandomLettersBot is a unique and exciting Telegram bot that allows users to send anonymous letters to one another. Simply send a message to the bot, and it will randomly select another user to receive your letter. No one will know who sent the letter or who received it, making it a fun and mysterious way to communicate with others. Whether you want to confess your secret crush, share a heartfelt message, or simply have a bit of fun, RandomLettersBot is the perfect platform. So why wait? Start sending anonymous letters today with RandomLettersBot!

Django Tutorials

Django Tutorials is an open source and free Persian translation of the official Django tutorials, providing a valuable resource for those who want to learn this powerful web framework in their native language. Django is a high-level Python web framework that encourages rapid development and clean, pragmatic design. With Django Tutorials, you can easily access step-by-step guides and exercises that will help you get started with Django and build your own web applications. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced developer seeking to expand your knowledge, Django Tutorials is the perfect place to start. So why wait? Check out Django Tutorials today and start building your Django skills!
